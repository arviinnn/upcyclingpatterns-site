Cookie Policy

This Cookie Policy explains how the Upcycling Patterns website may use cookies and similar technologies.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by websites. Local storage is a similar technology that stores information directly in your browser without sending it back to the server.

2. How This Website Uses Storage

The Upcycling Patterns website currently uses only browser local storage for two purposes:

  • Language preference — remembering whether you chose English or Turkish
  • Theme preference — remembering whether you chose the light or dark theme
  • Cookie consent — remembering that you've dismissed the cookie banner so we don't show it again

None of this information is sent to any server. It exists only in your browser and can be cleared at any time.

3. Essential Storage

The local storage items above are considered essential for the website to function correctly. They do not collect any sensitive personal information.

4. Analytics Cookies

This website does not currently use analytics cookies or trackers. If Google Analytics, Plausible, or another analytics service is added in the future, this policy will be updated and a consent prompt will be shown before any tracking begins.

5. Third-Party Services

The website may link to external platforms such as Instagram, X (Twitter), YouTube, LinkedIn, or form services like Web3Forms. These platforms may use their own cookies according to their own policies once you visit them. We have no control over their cookie practices.

6. Embedded Content

If the website embeds YouTube videos, those embeds use youtube-nocookie.com wherever possible to minimize tracking. Standard YouTube embeds may still set cookies once you click play.

7. Managing Cookies and Storage

You can manage or delete cookies and local storage through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to:

  • Block all cookies and storage
  • Block only third-party cookies
  • Delete existing cookies and storage on demand
  • Receive a notification before a cookie is set

Note that blocking essential storage may cause the language and theme switchers to forget your choice between visits.

8. Updates to This Policy

This Cookie Policy may be updated when new website features, analytics tools, or third-party services are added. The "Last updated" date below indicates the most recent revision.

9. Contact
