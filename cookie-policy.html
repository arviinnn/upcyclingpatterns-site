Cookie Policy

This Cookie Policy explains how the Upcycling Patterns Erasmus+ KA210-SCH project website uses cookies, local storage, and similar browser-based technologies.

Important: This website does not currently use tracking cookies, advertising cookies, or analytics cookies. It only uses essential browser storage to improve basic website functionality.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that websites may store on your device through your browser. They can help websites remember preferences, keep sessions active, or understand how a website is used.

2. What This Website Uses

The Upcycling Patterns website currently uses limited local browser storage for essential user preferences only.

  • Language preference: remembers whether you selected English or Turkish.
  • Theme preference: remembers whether you selected light mode or dark mode.
  • Cookie notice choice: remembers that you dismissed the cookie notice.

3. Essential Storage

These preferences are stored locally in your browser. They are used only to make the website easier to use and do not identify you personally.

4. Analytics and Tracking

This website does not currently use Google Analytics, Meta Pixel, advertising networks, behavioral tracking, or profiling tools.

5. Third-Party Services

Some website features may connect to third-party services when you actively use them. For example, the contact form may use Web3Forms to deliver messages to the project email address. Social media links may also take you to external platforms such as Instagram, X, YouTube, or LinkedIn.

6. Embedded Content

If videos or other embedded content are added in the future, those third-party platforms may use their own cookies or tracking technologies. Their cookie practices are controlled by the relevant third-party provider, not by this website.

7. How to Control Cookies

You can manage or delete cookies and local storage through your browser settings. You may also block cookies entirely, but some preferences such as language or theme may no longer be remembered.

8. Browser Storage Used by This Website

The website may store the following values in your browser:

  • siteLanguage: stores your selected website language.
  • siteTheme: stores your selected light or dark theme.
  • cookieConsentDismissed: stores whether the cookie notice has been closed.

9. Changes to This Cookie Policy

This Cookie Policy may be updated if the website adds new features, analytics, embedded media, or third-party services. Any important changes will be reflected on this page.

10. Contact

For questions about this Cookie Policy, you can contact us at: upcyclingpatterns@gmail.com
