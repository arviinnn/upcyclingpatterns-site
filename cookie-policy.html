Cookie Policy

This Cookie Policy explains how the Upcycling Patterns website may use cookies and similar technologies.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by websites. They help websites remember basic preferences and improve user experience.

2. How This Website Uses Cookies

The Upcycling Patterns website currently uses limited local browser storage for basic website functions such as language preference.

  • Remembering selected language preference
  • Improving website usability
  • Supporting basic website functionality

3. Essential Cookies

Essential cookies or local storage may be used to make the website work correctly. These do not collect sensitive personal information.

4. Analytics Cookies

This website does not currently use advanced analytics cookies. If Google Analytics or another analytics service is added in the future, this policy should be updated.

5. Third-Party Services

The website may link to external platforms such as Instagram, X, YouTube, LinkedIn, or form services. These platforms may use their own cookies according to their own policies.

6. Managing Cookies

You can manage or delete cookies through your browser settings. You can also block cookies, but some website features may not work correctly.

7. Updates to This Policy

This Cookie Policy may be updated when new website features, analytics tools, or third-party services are added.
