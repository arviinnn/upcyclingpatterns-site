Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how the Upcycling Patterns Erasmus+ KA210-SCH project website handles visitor information.

1. About This Website

Upcycling Patterns is an Erasmus+ KA210-SCH project focused on sustainability, environmental awareness, climate responsibility, and upcycling.

2. Information We May Collect

This website may collect limited information when visitors use the contact form or interact with the website.

  • Name and surname
  • Email address
  • Message subject
  • Message content

3. How We Use Information

Information sent through the contact form is used only to respond to messages, project-related questions, or partnership communication.

4. Contact Form

The contact form may use a third-party form service to deliver messages to the project email address. Submitted information is not used for advertising or commercial purposes.

5. Cookies and Analytics

This website may use basic cookies or analytics tools in the future to understand website traffic and improve user experience. If analytics tools are added, this policy may be updated.

6. Photos and Project Media

Project photos, videos, and visual materials may be published for Erasmus+ dissemination and visibility purposes. Personal data and student images should be used according to school permissions and applicable data protection rules.

7. Data Protection

We aim to respect GDPR and KVKK principles by collecting only necessary information and using it only for project communication and dissemination purposes.

8. External Links

This website may include links to social media platforms or partner institutions. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of external websites.

9. Contact

For privacy-related questions, you can contact us at: upcyclingpatterns@gmail.com
