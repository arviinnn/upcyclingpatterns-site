Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how the Upcycling Patterns Erasmus+ KA210-SCH project website handles visitor information.

1. About This Website

Upcycling Patterns is an Erasmus+ KA210-SCH project focused on sustainability, environmental awareness, climate responsibility, and upcycling.

2. Information We May Collect

This website may collect limited information when visitors use the contact form or interact with the website.

  • Name and surname
  • Email address
  • Message subject
  • Message content
  • Selected language and theme preferences, stored locally in your browser only

3. How We Use Information

Information sent through the contact form is used only to respond to messages, project-related questions, or partnership communication. It is never sold, shared with advertisers, or used for any commercial purpose.

4. Contact Form

The contact form may use a third-party form delivery service, Web3Forms, to route messages to the project email address. Submitted information is transmitted over HTTPS and is not used for advertising.

5. Local Storage

The website stores your language preference, theme preference, and cookie notice choice in your browser's local storage. This data stays on your device. No tracking cookies or analytics services are currently used by this website.

6. Photos and Project Media

Project photos, videos, and visual materials may be published for Erasmus+ dissemination and visibility purposes. Personal data and student images are used only with appropriate school permissions and in line with applicable data protection rules.

7. Data Protection

We aim to respect GDPR and KVKK principles by collecting only necessary information and using it only for project communication and dissemination purposes. You may request the deletion of any personal data we hold by contacting us.

8. External Links

This website may include links to social media platforms or partner institutions. We are not responsible for the privacy practices, content, or security of external websites.

9. Your Rights

You have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of any personal data we hold about you. To exercise these rights, please contact us at the address below.

10. Contact

For privacy-related questions or data requests, you can contact us at: upcyclingpatterns@gmail.com
